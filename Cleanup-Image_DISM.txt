::Using the /StartComponenetCleanup switch, previous versions of updated components will be immediately deleted (without a 30 day grace period).
::Using the /StartCOmponentCleanup and /ResetBase switches, removes all superseded versions of every component in the component store.
::Using the /SPSuperseded switch,  removes any backup components needed for uninstallation of service packs.


Dism.exe /online /Cleanup-Image /StartComponentCleanup

Dism.exe /online /Cleanup-Image /StartComponentCleanup /ResetBase

Dism.exe /online /Cleanup-Image /SPSuperseded
