::To Slipstream Updates into a WIM file or Windows ISO, use these commands as an outline.

Dism /Mount-WIM /WimFile:C:\Win7SP1x64\sources\install.wim /Name:"Windows 7 Professional" /MountDir:C:\Win7SP1x64\offline

Dism /Image:C:\Win7SP1x64\offline /Add-Package /PackagePath:C:\Updatesx64\Windows6.1-KB3020369-x64.msu

Dism /Image:C:\Win7SP1x64\offline /Add-Package /PackagePath:C:\updatesx64\windows6.1-kb3125574-v4-x64_2dafb1d203c8964239af3048b5dd4b1264cd93b9.msu

Dism /Image:C:\Win7SP1x64\offline /Add-Package /PackagePath:C:\updatesx64\windows6.1-kb4038777-x64_193d527f3487640fc18f1f596c0434e64cd20c51.msu

Dism /Unmount-WIM /MountDir:C:\Win7SP1x64\offline /Commit
